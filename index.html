Major Sports

Baseball

Major League Baseball has 30 teams. It is split into two leagues, the American and National league. Each league is split into three divisions, East, Central, and West.The National league teams include the Mets and Nationals. The Yankees, Orioles and Texas Rangers are 3 teams in the American League. The recent champions are the Chicago Cubs.
